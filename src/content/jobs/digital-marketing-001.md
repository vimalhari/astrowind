## Role overview

You’ll plan, execute, and improve digital marketing programs for international clients. The role is hands-on across SEO, content distribution, paid acquisition, and analytics—focused on measurable growth.

## What you’ll work on

- SEO: keyword research, on-page recommendations, content briefs, and audits
- Paid campaigns: Google Ads, Meta Ads, and (when relevant) LinkedIn Ads
- Social: planning and execution across platforms with clear performance goals
- Analytics: GA4, Search Console, and reporting that drives action
- CRO: landing-page improvements and A/B testing to lift conversions

## What success looks like

- Improved qualified traffic and lead volume over time
- Clear reporting with insights and next-step recommendations
- Strong collaboration with content and design for better creatives/pages

## Interview process

1. Intro call (experience + fit)
2. Channel deep-dive (SEO/paid/analytics)
3. Short case study (audit a campaign or landing page)
4. Final discussion (ways of working + offer)

## How to apply

Send your resume along with:

- 1–2 examples of results you’ve driven (metrics + context)
- Any dashboards/reports you’ve built (screenshots are fine)
- Certifications (Google Ads/GA4/Meta) if available
